We are looking for a senior developer to join our product team. You'll work closely with all different team members at Iron Gaming (IG) to lead the development of our new product. The ideal candidate must have real-world engineering experience with successful products shipped.

As one of our core values, working as a No Ego Doer is an important part of being a developer at IG. You'll need to be aware of when you have assumptions, and be disciplined about working with other team members in order to test and confirm your ideas.

About Iron Gaming

The mission of IG is to establish a tiered ranking and scoring system for gamers that allows them to compete against similarly skilled opponents, and to bring esports to a wider audience.




What we value

Trust, integrity, teamwork, respect, positivity and compassion are just a few of IG’s cultural values. We are looking for a person who understands this culture and will thrive within a team of people passionate about self-improvement, honesty and openness. It is our belief that members of a great team embrace clear communication, transparency and open-mindedness.

Senior Developer / Team Lead

  • Team-oriented mindset. It may sound cliche, but we are very much a team of peers; being able to work towards the best interests of the team, regardless of personal preference, is important.
