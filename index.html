We are looking for a senior developer to join our product team. You'll work closely with all different team members at Iron Gaming (IG) to lead the development of our new product. The ideal candidate must have real-world engineering experience with successful products shipped.

As one of our core values, working as a "No Ego Doer" is an important part of being a developer at IG. You'll need to be aware of when you have assumptions, and be disciplined about working with other team members in order to test and confirm your ideas.

Who are we any way?

Real competitive play, is our play.

Gaming is our passion and creating a new space in which gamers can thrive is our modus operandi. Iron Gaming (IG) is a web application that allows for a unique experience for all gamers. Our application has a tiered ranking and scoring system for gamers that allows every player the ability to compete against similarly skilled opponents, and a focus to bring esports to a wider audience. Piqued your competitive nature? Perfect. You might just be in the right place. To add to the thrill of the game, we are specialists in hosting online and live gaming tournaments, and we have a heck of a time doing it.




What do we value?

Among creating a thriving environment for gamers to compete in, our approach to making this a thriving work environment is the second half of our secret sauce (and we don’t mind spilling the beans). At IG, we combine a few values like trust, positivity and compassion to the mix when bringing our team together.

Senior Developer / Team Lead

If you are a transparent communicator with a knack for leading talented product team members, this is the position for you.

Roles/Responsibilities

  • Technical leadership is your passion and you are no noob to writing and maintaining web applications, back-end, front-end and server side.
  • Your development experience runs really deep and you have a good deal of experience in data modeling.
  • You have a deep appreciation (love) of videogames and understanding of multiplayer / competitive play
  • You love the product development process, from implementation to release
  • Documenting the things you build is a must. Some folks call you a process junkie, others OCD, but either of those comments are really just compliments to you
  • You love technology and your technical chops are as sharp as your Kung Fu
  • You’ve built several products in your career with an above average success rate for all of them. In fact, you’ve made a bit of a science out of getting great products out the door
  • Building beautiful things and working with creative experts is like music to your ears
  • Mentoring other folks comes natural to you and you enjoy the collaboration process
  • Deadlines don’t freak you out, they motivate you and keep you and your team on task

  • 3-5 years of experience with mobile APIs
  • Preferred languages/frameworks: Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, Node, Angular (or another JS framework)
  • Understand the importance of UX and UI and experience working with design teams
  • Experience in a startup environment
